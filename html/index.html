Our Mission

Our mission is to promote learning of the Japanese language, while serving delicious Japanese tea, along with other snacks and beverages. The point of this club to promote tolerance and unity between different cultures, by exposing people to new and different lifestyles.

Language Exchange

One of the mains points of this club is to promote the exchange of language between internation students and American local students. This is done mainly through conversations and games. Be prepared to learn something new every time you come to our club.

Positive Social Interactions

With this club, we want to provide a fun place to exchange cultures and languages. By providing a fun place to hang out, we strive to promote positive social interactions between students who come from different backgrounds and walks of life.

Enjoying Tea

Finally, our club has a strong focus around enjoying tea! It is well known that meals and drinks can bring people closer together despite lack of cultural differences, and we hope to take advantage of that by providing delicious tea every club meeting.
